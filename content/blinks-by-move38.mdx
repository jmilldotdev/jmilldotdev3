The Blinks System is 9 of the most popular Blinks games and everything you need to start playing today. All of these games play great with 9 Blinks out of the box and expand in difficulty and fun when combined with more Blinks from other sets.
 What's in the Box

    9 Blinks Games
    2 x Sushi Roll carrying case
    1 x Game Instructions

Blinks is an Open Platform and Open-source so your Blinks will continually expand to play new games as you introduce new Blinks.
