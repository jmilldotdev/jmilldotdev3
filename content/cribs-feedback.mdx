> Confusing, Repeated, Insightful, Boring, Surprinsing

Standard for giving helpful constructive feedback on writing.

All have a stragihtforward writing antidote:
- Confusing - Rewrite
- Repeated - Delete
- Insightful - Expand
- Boring - Rephrase or Remove
- Surprising - Follow the thread
