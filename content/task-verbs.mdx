Define your tasks with the handful of verbs that define your typical set of actions. Helps to match work to mood, [[mood-based productivity is the key to effective modern knowledge work]].

Example: add, buy, call, email, list, listen to, online research, plan, read, review, schedule, try out, waiting for, watch, write
